Frequently Asked Questions

Is the cheek swab painful?
Not at all. It feels like brushing the inside of your cheek with a soft cotton tip. Takes about 30 seconds. No blood, no needles, no discomfort whatsoever.
How accurate are the results?
Our lab uses clinical-grade genotyping technology with 99.9% accuracy. Every result is backed by peer-reviewed scientific research and validated by our medical team.
How long until I get my results?
Standard processing takes 2-3 weeks from when we receive your sample. Premium and Complete plans include priority processing with results in approximately 10 business days.
Is my genetic data safe?
Absolutely. Your data is encrypted with 256-bit AES encryption, stored in compliance with UAE data protection regulations, and never shared with third parties. You can request deletion at any time.
Can I share results with my doctor?
Yes. Your dashboard includes a downloadable PDF report that you can share with your healthcare provider. We also offer direct consultations with our certified genetic counselors.
