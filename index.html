Common Questions

Everything you need to know

Still have questions? Contact us anytime.

Is the cheek swab painful?
Not at all. It feels like brushing the inside of your cheek with a soft cotton tip. Takes 30 seconds. No blood, no needles.
How accurate are the results?
Our lab uses clinical-grade genotyping with 99.9% accuracy. Every result is backed by peer-reviewed research and validated by our medical team.
How long until I get my results?
Standard processing takes 2-3 weeks. Premium plans include priority processing with results in approximately 10 business days.
Is my genetic data safe?
Your data is encrypted with 256-bit AES, stored in compliance with UAE PDPL regulations, and never shared with third parties. You can request deletion at any time.
Can I share results with my doctor?
Yes — your dashboard includes a downloadable PDF you can share with any healthcare provider. We also offer genetic counselor consultations.
Do you ship across all Emirates?
Yes. Free shipping to Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Sharjah, Ajman, Ras Al Khaimah, Fujairah, and Umm Al Quwain. Kits arrive in 1-2 business days.
